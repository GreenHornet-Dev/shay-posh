🏅 Meet Shay!

✨ Graphic designer by passion. Perfectly Posh advocate by choice. ✨

Hi, I'm Shay Hewitt! I've spent years with a creative eye — graphic design has been a huge part of my life, shaping how I see color, beauty, and the little details that make something truly special. That love of design carried right over when I discovered Perfectly Posh.

Posh caught my attention because the products are as beautifully crafted as they are effective — good, clean ingredients, gorgeous scents, and real results. From the moment I tried my first hand creme I was hooked! Now I get to combine two things I love: sharing great self-care finds and bringing a little creativity to everything I do.

I share my Posh favorites with friends, family, and anyone who stumbles across this little corner of the internet. When you shop through my link you get the exact same great products and prices as the main site — and you support a real person who genuinely loves what she sells. 💕
